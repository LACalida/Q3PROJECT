We care about YOUR safety

Read these tips and reminders to keep your trip safe and fun!



🚆 Before Your Trip

✅ Check the Schedule – MRT-3 operates from 4:30 AM to 10:30 PM, but schedules may change, so check updates online.

✅ Prepare Your Card or Fare – Use a Beep Card for faster entry or have exact fare ready for single-journey tickets.

✅ Dress Comfortably – MRT-3 can get crowded, so wear light clothing and comfy shoes.



🚉 At the Station

✅ Follow Security Procedures – Bags are inspected at the entrance; avoid bringing prohibited items.

✅ Use the Right Lane – There are special queues for PWDs, seniors, and pregnant women.

✅ Stay on the Right Side of Escalators – Let others pass on the left.



🚋 Inside the Train

✅ Move to the Center – Avoid blocking doors to let others in and out smoothly.

✅ Give Seats to Those in Need – Prioritize seats for seniors, PWDs, and pregnant passengers.

✅ No Eating or Drinking – Help keep the train clean for everyone.



📢 Additional Reminders

🚫 Beware of Pickpockets – Keep your bag in front of you and secure your valuables.

📍 Be Aware of Your Stop – Stations are announced, so get ready before arriving at your destination.

🕒 Avoid Rush Hours – Peak times are 7:00-9:00 AM and 5:00-8:00 PM on weekdays.
